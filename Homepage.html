The Pinball Primer

No nonsense summaries of pinball table layouts and rules to get you on your way, whether you're playing casually on location, playing in a tournament, or just want to read about some table tips and strategies.
This site is a work in progress, and table rulesets are constantly being added.
Currently: 120 rulesets and counting.

If you have requests, questions, or corrections, email me at aquaticespeon@gmail.com or message me on Twitter, Discord, or Instagram (solar_espeon everywhere).

For more about the story behind this website and an FAQ, click here.
For a glossary of pinball terms, click here.

Please enjoy!

Note: tables with a title whose first word is "The" are sorted by the second word in their name, such as "Amazing Spider-Man, The".
Abbreviations:
EM = electromechanical
SS = early solid state
AN = alphanumeric
DMD = dot matrix display
